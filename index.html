A Blessed Stay in Vrindavan

✦

Hotel Srijan is a premium boutique hotel dedicated to providing pilgrims and travelers with a clean, peaceful, and comfortable haven in the sacred town of Vrindavan, Mathura. Strategically located opposite Fogla Ashram, our property guarantees easy transit to the region's spiritual epicenters.

Whether you are visiting for a family pilgrimage, spiritual retreat, or a weekend getaway, our spacious rooms, welcoming hospitality, on-site dining, and modern services will make your trip memorable.

Nearby Holy Attractions

✦
1.3 km • 5 Min Drive / 12 Min Walk

Prem Mandir

The temple of divine love, constructed of Italian white marble. Famous for its intricate carvings and stunning musical light fountain show in the evening.

1.5 km • 6 Min Drive / 15 Min Walk

Shri Banke Bihari Ji

One of India's most highly revered temples dedicated to Lord Krishna (Thakur Ji). Known for its joyful spiritual ambiance and curtain swings during darshan.

1.2 km • 5 Min Drive / 15 Min Walk

ISKCON Vrindavan

Also known as Sri Sri Krishna Balaram Mandir, this tranquil sanctuary is globally famous for its ecstatic 24-hour kirtans and beautiful white marble courtyard.

9.0 km • 25 Min Drive

Krishna Janmabhoomi

Located in neighboring Mathura, this highly sacred temple complex is built around the prison cell where Lord Krishna was born. A must-visit pilgrimage spot.
